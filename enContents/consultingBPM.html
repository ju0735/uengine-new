Our Services

Consulting Services

uEngine provides consulting services for all areas related to uEngine.
This service comprehensively covers product installation, usage, customization, integration with uEngine BPM, and application of open-source SW related to uEngine.

BPMS system consulting

uEngine provides consulting services for building uEngine BPM (BPMS). This service offers simultaneous online and offline support, ensuring that projects are carried out smoothly with the participation of skilled engineers. It covers all areas from process analysis and design to training.

Furthermore, we provide consulting services for analysis, design, problem-solving, technical support, and training for project progression. Any issues or questions raised regarding the project are promptly received and addressed, and consulting time is agreed upon mutually. We offer support and answers through remote support tools such as phone, email, and remote control.

Open source SW application (uEngine BPM integration) service

We provide services for installation and integration with open-source SW such as Alfresco, BI Mondrian, and Liferay, which are linked to the uEngine product.

Please do not hesitate to contact me if you need any more information and questions.
